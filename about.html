Our Vision

At Odysey Architects & Associates, OAA is actively engaged in providing contextually appropriate solutions within the increasingly changing world. Established in Navi Mumbai on 2012, apart from providing Architectural solutions, OAA provides service in Project Feasibility and Project Management & Consultancy.

We also provide service in Landscape and Interior Designing and provide Research and Development prior to any project for betterment of Client. Being a part of service industry, delivery on time is our commitment. Quality over Quantity is our motive.

Leadership

Ketan Sawant :: Proprietor

An experience of above 7 Yrs of Architectural practice and an unmatched knowledge in Real Estate and Finance and with an highly prestigious Post graduation degree from NICMAR, Ketan Sawant has worked in most of the parts of Maharashtra with multiple Corporation. In his earlier days, he has worked with reputed firms in the industry like Dimensions and Ahuja Builders and Developers and later he has worked as an Partner for Vastukala Architects Pvt. Ltd.

Finally in year 2012, he started his own organization of Odyssey Architects and Associates.
